Acceptable Use Policy

Last Updated: June 4, 2025

1. Introduction

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of CountryMax Email Delivery services. By using our services, you agree to comply with this AUP. Violations may result in suspension or termination of your account.

2. Prohibited Content

You may not use our services to send, or facilitate the sending of, any content that:

  • Is illegal, harmful, or promotes illegal activities
  • Contains sexually explicit material or adult content
  • Promotes violence, hate speech, or discrimination
  • Infringes on intellectual property rights
  • Contains false or misleading information
  • Promotes illegal drugs, weapons, or regulated substances
  • Contains malware, viruses, or malicious code

3. Prohibited Activities

You may not use our services for:

  • Sending unsolicited commercial email (spam)
  • Harvesting email addresses without consent
  • Phishing or fraudulent activities
  • Impersonating others or spoofing identities
  • Denial of service attacks
  • High-risk activities without our prior written consent
  • Violating CAN-SPAM, GDPR, CASL, or other email regulations

4. Sending Requirements

All emails sent through our service must:

  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
  • Include a valid physical postal address
  • Contain a clear and functional unsubscribe mechanism
  • Accurately identify the sender in the "From" field
  • Have truthful and non-deceptive subject lines
  • Be sent to recipients who have provided explicit consent

5. List Hygiene Requirements

You must maintain clean and up-to-date email lists:

  • Remove hard bounces immediately
  • Suppress unsubscribed addresses permanently
  • Remove inactive addresses (no engagement in 6 months)
  • Regularly validate email addresses
  • Maintain documentation of consent for all recipients

6. Performance Thresholds

To maintain good sending reputation, you must:

  • Maintain complaint rates below 0.1% (1 complaint per 1,000 emails)
  • Maintain bounce rates below 5%
  • Maintain spam trap hits below 0.01%
  • Ensure unsubscribe rates below industry averages

Accounts exceeding these thresholds may be suspended.

7. Security Requirements

You must:

  • Secure your account credentials
  • Use API keys and SMTP credentials securely
  • Implement DMARC, DKIM, and SPF for your sending domains
  • Promptly report any security incidents
  • Use TLS encryption for all email transmissions

8. Monitoring and Enforcement

We actively monitor all email traffic for compliance with this AUP:

  • Automated systems scan content and headers
  • Manual reviews of high-volume senders
  • Reputation monitoring with major ISPs
  • Feedback loop monitoring with mailbox providers

Violations will result in warnings, suspension, or termination.

9. Reporting Violations

To report violations of this AUP, contact us at:

Email: abuse@countrymaxemail.com
Phone: +1 (800) 555-ABUSE

We investigate all reports within 24 business hours.

10. Policy Updates

We may update this AUP at any time. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
